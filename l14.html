Louis Fourteen

Creating an ultra-premium chat-based concierge app.

role/

Lead UX & Visual Design

agency/

Freelance

industry/

Concierge

year/

2018

overview/

Louis Fourteen (L14) is a Dubai-based boutique lifestyle management company allowing its members to get the most out of life by organizing bespoke travel experiences, sourcing luxury goods, providing access to special events, and arranging personal shopping and styling services.

As the sole UX and visual designer on the project, I designed L14’s first mobile app and helped strategize the best way to curate and present offers to customers while optimizing for effortless conversions. The app is now being licensed by Mastercard as a concierge service for World Elite cardholders.

Ask Louis

Completing a request requires back and forth between Lifestyle Specialists and clientele as they gather preferences and personal details (think booking a week-long getaway for 5 to Maldives). Insights found that members were too busy for phone calls, emails got lost in inboxes, and this scattered communication was hard to keep track of for Lifestyle Specialists while the personal touch of a phone call led to more conversions. These insights guided us to make the app chat-based, standardizing communication and humanizing the purchasing process.

Ask Louis, the core feature of the app, was designed to allow clientele to chat casually and naturally build a relationship with their Lifestyle Specialist while providing an easy, unobtrusive way for Specialists to reach out to members to gather any additional information they may need.

Open Offers

Louis Fourteen wanted a way for members to buy pre-defined offers; however, most offers require additional information before they can be purchased. For this we created open offers, like reserving a table at a Michelin Star restaurant or planning a trip to the floating lantern festival, that route the client back to Ask Louis for the Lifestyle Specialist to gather the additional information required before booking.

When a Serendipity offer requires additional info before being purchased, the member is taken to Ask Louis, where their Lifestyle Specialist follows up to gather the missing details.

Serendipity Curation

We also noticed an issue within the Serendipity section: members were being served offers that were not relevant to them. To curate offers specifically for each member, we created a tagging system that used data captured in user onboarding, info added by Lifestyle Specialists, and app usage to present relevant offers based on location, interests, upcoming travel, marital status, etc.

We created a tagging system to present members with offers that were relevant to them based on data we had collected over the course of their relationship with their Lifestyle Specialist and app usage.

App Launch Brings Growth

The advantages of chat-based e-commerce allowed us to create a user journey that is much more efficient from wish to fulfillment for both the customer and L14. This led to the app exceeding expectations and has required L14 to expand their team of Lifestyle Specialists as they continue to take on more members and fill more requests than ever before. Mastercard’s UAE division has also noticed the benefits of this service and are now licensing the app to serve as concierge for its top-tier cardholders.

I designed the app to be easily scalable with subtle updates for licensing opportunities, with Mastercard UAE being the first to license and use the app and L14’s services for World Elite cardholder concierge.
