ALEXA

furniture design

A chaise lounge designed to bring something fresh to the market with timeless style and unexampled versatility. Alexa was inspired by the movie Blade Runner and its juxtaposition of 1980s landscapes and futuristic creations.

The Alexa chaise marries mid-century modern forms with contemporary materials. The hard, steel exterior provides a great contrast to the plush interior while the nickel-plated legs visually lighten the stark form. The pure metal surface and beautiful reveal in the corner make for a stunning back side if used as a room divider. If you wish to add a pop of color in your room, the steel can easily be powder coated any color you desire.
