VOYAGER

interface design

A head-up display and digital companion helping motorcyclists get the most of their ride. By greatly improving riders' safety and enjoyment of the ride, this duo brings the world of motorcycles up to speed with that of passenger cars. VOYAGER's app-based community provides the perfect outlet to share and uncover the hottest rides around while the synced HUD puts your bike's instrument cluster and smartphone's features directly into the environment in which you are riding.
